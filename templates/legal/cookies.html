Cookie Policy

FormaSpace uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve website functionality.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to recognise returning visitors and measure performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics: Help us understand visitor behaviour (Google Analytics).
  • Performance: Improve site speed and usability.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. Some features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.
